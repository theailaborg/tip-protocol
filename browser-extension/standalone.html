TIP Protocol

Trust Identity Protocol

A blockchain system that lets creators prove who made their content and whether AI was involved - permanently and verifiably.

The 4 Origin Labels

OH
Original Human
You created this entirely without AI assistance.
AA
AI-Assisted
You led the work; AI helped edit, improve, or refine it.
AG
AI-Generated
AI generated this; you prompted and curated it.
MX
Mixed
A combination. Safe default when unsure.
Safe rule: Over-declaring AI involvement carries zero penalty. Declaring OH when AI generated the content risks trust score reduction.

Choosing Your Origin Code

OH - Original Human

You wrote, filmed, or created this entirely without AI tools contributing to the content.

AA - AI-Assisted

You led the creative work. AI tools helped edit, improve, generate a thumbnail, or enhance your original content.

AG - AI-Generated

AI generated the primary content. You provided prompts, curated the output, and published it.

MX - Mixed

A combination of human and AI contributions. Safe default when the balance is unclear.

Safe rule: When in doubt, declare more AI. Declaring AA when it's OH costs nothing. Declaring OH when it's AG can reduce your trust score.
